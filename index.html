What Are Enchiladas Suizas?

Enchiladas suizas are a creamy, cheesy, and flavorful Mexican dish made with shredded chicken wrapped in tortillas and topped with a rich green tomatillo sauce. They’re known for their comforting taste and Swiss‑inspired creaminess.

Baked enchiladas suizas in a glass dish.

Fun Facts About Enchiladas Suizas

  • Originated in Mexico City
  • “Suizas” refers to the creamy, Swiss‑style sauce
  • Usually made with tomatillos, crema, and melted cheese
  • Popular in restaurants and home kitchens
